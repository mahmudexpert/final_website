The Game

The Beginning


Set in the year 1200 on distant planet in the Aura system, Lunhelm is at war. The planet has been targeted for harvesting by the Cryptic monsters, putting all their lives and worldly possessions into rebut. The king, Aldrich V has called upon the greatest champions from his kingdoms to help repel the monster hoards.


The cryptic monsters are thing of nightmares. They utilize the divine powers of earth, water, fire , light, and darkness, making them unstoppable to those without the knowledge.

Taking On The Challenge


Heroes who decide to take on the challenge of the Cryptic hoards will need to play with strategy and instincts by playing against the elements and leading Lunhelm to victory.


There is over 12 heroes awaiting your command, all having different skills and abilities which can be enhanced and powered up by the legendary elemental shards.

Where Does Zilliqa Fit In?


Zilliqa will play a vital roll in the Heroes of Lunhelm game. Players can purchase shards, weapons and so much more with the native HOL token, with all purchases from the HOL system being burnt.


The game itself is free to play, but users looking to enhance their experience can buy amazing perks on the HOL marketplace.
